Flood alert is for minor flooding
  • shallow flooding of roads and footpaths
  • low impact areas such as fields or farmland

Flood warning is for flooding
  • properties are expected to flood
  • higher impact areas such as major roads or infrastructure

Severe flood warning is for severe flooding
  • there is a risk to life or significant disruption for people
  • flood waters may be deep or fast flowing
  • evacuations may be required
  • emergency services and local authorities will be heavily involved
